About us

SilverNote is a non-genre specific music label and creative enterprise hub based at North East Scotland College.  From the company structure and the job roles, to the signing, development and distribution of creative products and projects, SilverNote is real-world enterprise, focusing on creative development and, specifically, new music in the North East of Scotland.

 

SilverNote creates opportunities to work with either the creation or administration and enterprise of creative assets - the two roles which underpin the creative industries as a whole.

SilverNote reaches outwards to into the local community to engage and inspire creative professionals and other external organisations as a hub for creative enterprise, and a provider of creative services, products and projects.

In addition, SilverNote disseminates artists work globally - raising the profile of new music and artists and creative enterprise in the North East of Scotland.

Shannon Feely

Shannon Feely is a 20-year-old singer songwriter from Aberdeen. She has taken part in various gigs over the years in a range of venues including The Lemon Tree in Aberdeen and Airdrie Town Hall.

Shannon wrote and recorded a 4-track EP in 2017 and is working on developing that this year in the hope of releasing some of her music later in the year with original songs such as; ‘Falling Apart’, ‘City that never Sleeps’ and ‘I’ve got to be Me’ featuring in this project.

RedKid

RedKid, is a new and upcoming vocal artist but prefers the colour blue. He started sing professionally three years ago, after being complemented on his voice.

He currently has two EPs and is working on an album. RedKid’s long term goal is to be a song writer.

Glass Monkey

Starting as a cover band in 2014, Glass Monkey moved onto writing their own songs.

They've been playing small gigs around Aberdeen City and Shire, and have a passion for playing live gigs.

Glass Monkey's influences range throughout pop punk and rock music, taking inspiration from artists such as The Foo Fighters, Neck Deep, Waterparks and All Time Low.
