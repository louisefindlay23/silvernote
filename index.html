About us

SilverNote is a non-genre specific music label and creative enterprise hub based at North East Scotland College.  From the company structure and the job roles, to the signing, development and distribution of creative products and projects, SilverNote is real-world enterprise, focusing on creative development and, specifically, new music in the North East of Scotland.  SilverNote creates opportunities to work with either the creation or administration and enterprise of creative assets - the two roles which underpin the creative industries as a whole. SilverNote reaches outwards to into the local community to engage and inspire creative professionals and other external organisations as a hub for creative enterprise, and a provider of creative services, products and projects.  In addition, SilverNote disseminates artists work globally - raising the profile of new music and artists and creative enterprise in the North East of Scotland.

Contact us for more info:

RedKid

RedKid, is a new and upcoming vocal artist but prefers the colour blue. RedKid started to play the guitar at the age of 13, then moved on to playing the saxophone and piano. RedKid started sing professionally three years ago, after being complemented on his voice. He currently has to two Eps and is working on an album. RedKid’s long term goal is to be a song writing. RedKid’s main music influences are Lady Gaga and Marlin Manson. He also takes influences from different vocalists. The name RedKid came from a friend saying “You look like a RedKid.” While his hair was dyed red. The name stuck and we have RedKid.
